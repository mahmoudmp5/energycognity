What is a Grid?

National Grid

Large interconnected systems ensuring reliable electricity supply.

Microgrid

Localized systems that can run independently, often with renewables.

Smart Grid

Modernized grids using digital technology for efficiency and reliability.

Off-Grid

Independent systems for remote areas or energy independence.

Mini-Grid

Mid-sized systems for small communities not connected to the national grid.

Hybrid Grid

Combination of traditional grid and renewable/local generation.
