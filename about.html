About Enercognity

Enercognity is an AI-powered energy optimization company. We build devices that act as brokers between prosumers and utilities, analyzing weather conditions, grid status, and user patterns to suggest smarter energy usage. Our mission is to reduce energy costs, promote renewable adoption, and make the energy grid smarter and more sustainable.

Our Mission

Enercognity is dedicated to helping individuals and organizations monitor, analyze, and optimize their energy consumption. Our goal is to contribute to a sustainable future by promoting energy efficiency and reducing waste.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate engineers and innovators committed to creating solutions that make a difference. With expertise in IoT, data analytics, and energy systems, we aim to transform the way energy is utilized.
